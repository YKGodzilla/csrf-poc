CSRF Attack Simulation

This page simulates a malicious website attempting to change the user's email on the banking demo site without their consent.

Important: The target site at http://localhost:3000/profile/email accepts POST requests without a CSRF token or origin check.

The hidden form will submit to the banking site immediately. If the user is authenticated, the email will change.

Use this demo after opening the /profile page on the banking site to observe the vulnerable flow.
